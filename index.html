// Questions

Things people ask us.

What exactly is GEO and why does it matter now? ›
GEO — Generative Engine Optimisation — is the practice of improving how your brand appears inside AI-generated answers. When someone asks ChatGPT "what's the best gym near me?" or Gemini "which solicitor should I use?", those models generate answers from everything they've learned. GEO makes sure your brand is part of that answer.
Can you guarantee my brand will appear in AI answers? ›
No — and anyone who promises that is misleading you. Generative AI models are probabilistic. What we do is systematically improve every signal that influences outputs — structured content, entity consistency, citation authority, schema markup — and track the improvement monthly. We promise measurable progress, not a guaranteed position.
How do you measure AI visibility without scraping ChatGPT? ›
We use official developer APIs — OpenAI, Google Gemini, and Perplexity — not the consumer interfaces. These give consistent, reproducible results and are designed for exactly this kind of structured testing. We run a fixed set of buyer prompts in a controlled environment and log every output. Structured sampling, not scraping.
What sectors do you work with? ›
We work with any brand where customers use AI to make decisions — fintech, e-commerce, hospitality, professional services, health & wellness, SaaS, marketplaces, and more. If your customers ask AI "which [your category] should I use?" — you need Seren. We build custom prompt sets for each sector and market.
How long before I see results? ›
AEO improvements (Google snippets, AI Overviews) typically show in 4–8 weeks. GEO — LLM visibility — takes longer as models update on their own schedules. Expect meaningful signal after 60–90 days. That's why our Monthly plan has a 3-month minimum. The audit on day one gives you a baseline; every month shows the trend.
What does the free audit actually include? ›
A full 6-sheet Excel report: AI Visibility Score (0–100), every prompt result across ChatGPT, Gemini and Perplexity, sentiment breakdown, gap analysis showing which competitors are winning and why, and a prioritised action plan with specific page titles and content briefs generated by AI. Delivered within 48 hours. No commitment required.
